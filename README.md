# NextMove - Startup Pitch Platform

**Where Bold Ideas Meet the Right Eyes**

A full-stack platform connecting visionary founders with investors who believe in them. Built with the MERN stack, NextMove enables startups to showcase their pitches and investors to discover opportunities.

## Features
### For Founders
- **Upload Pitches** - Share startup decks, videos, or documents
- **My Pitches** - Manage all your submitted pitches
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track views, engagement, and ratings
- **Feedback System** - Receive structured investor feedback
- **Profile Management** - Maintain comprehensive business profile

### For Investors
- **Browse Pitches** - Discover startups by category, tags, or funding goals
- **Rate & Review** - Provide ratings (1-5 stars) and detailed feedback
- **Saved Pitches** - Bookmark interesting opportunities
- **Investment Tracking** - Monitor portfolio and commitments
- **Direct Contact** - Connect with founders instantly
